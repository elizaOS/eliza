## A. Critical Assessment

### Finding 1: ELIZA_1_HF_REPO was wrong — all downloads would 404

The most critical bug found: `ELIZA_1_HF_REPO = "elizalabs/eliza-1"` across
the entire codebase. The actual HF org is `elizaos`. Every download URL
generated by the runtime (`buildHuggingFaceResolveUrl`, `downloadRemotePath`,
etc.) would produce a 404. This is a complete blocker for the "just works"
install path.

**Fixed in commit `cd79fe1186`** — 15 files updated across TypeScript, Python,
and test fixtures. Catalog test (was failing) now passes.

### Finding 2: 27b-1m tier is entirely missing from HF (hardware-gated)

No bundle exists at `elizaos/eliza-1/bundles/27b-1m/`. This is a legitimate
hardware blocker: the tier requires 160 GB RAM (H200 cluster). Marked
`"pending"` in `ELIZA_1_TIER_PUBLISH_STATUS` so the recommender correctly
excludes it from first-run suggestions.

### Finding 3: Voice sub-model repos do not exist

10 planned separate HF repos (`elizaos/eliza-1-voice-speaker-encoder`,
`elizaos/eliza-1-voice-turn-detector`, etc.) are not created. The voice
sub-model weights ARE present inside the main bundle (under `asr/`, `vad/`,
`tts/`) — just not in separate repos. The `voice-models.ts` architecture is
aspirational. The bundled approach works for "just works" install.

### Finding 4: Vision mmproj missing for 0_8b and 2b

`catalog.ts` marks `hasVision: true` for 0_8b and 2b, but the HF bundles have
empty `files.vision` arrays. Since the manifest is authoritative (downloader
reads what's in the manifest, not the catalog), vision is functionally
disabled on these tiers. No crash — just feature absent.

### Finding 5: Voice preset is a placeholder

`cache/voice-preset-default.bin` is 1052 bytes (zero-filled placeholder) on
all bundles. The real samantha preset from I6/I7 was not pushed (I7 eval
regressed; I6 omnivoice freeze had the samantha preset as ELZ2 v2 but the
push to HF was not executed in Wave 2). Voice works but defaults to af_bella.
