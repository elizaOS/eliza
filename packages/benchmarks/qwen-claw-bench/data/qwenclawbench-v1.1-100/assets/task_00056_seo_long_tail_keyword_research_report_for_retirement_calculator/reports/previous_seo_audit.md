# SEO Audit Report: Insurance Calculator Product Line

**Prepared by:** Digital Marketing Team  
**Date:** September 12, 2024  
**Client:** InsureCalc Division  
**Audit Period:** Q2–Q3 2024  

---

## Executive Summary

This SEO audit evaluates the organic search performance of our insurance calculator product line, including the **Insurance Premium Calculator**, **Term Life Insurance Estimator**, and **Health Insurance Comparison Tool**. Overall organic traffic to insurance calculator pages declined 8.3% quarter-over-quarter, primarily due to increased competition from NerdWallet and Policygenius in the health insurance comparison space.

Key findings indicate that our term life insurance calculator pages are underperforming relative to search demand, while the health insurance comparison tool has strong technical SEO but weak content depth compared to competitors.

## Keyword Gap Analysis

### Opportunities Identified

1. **"cheap term life insurance calculator no medical exam"** — 1,400 monthly searches, no dedicated page
2. **"health insurance marketplace comparison tool 2024"** — 2,800 monthly searches, competitor-dominated
3. **"insurance premium calculator for small business owners"** — 890 monthly searches, low competition
4. **"compare whole life vs term life insurance calculator"** — 1,100 monthly searches, partial coverage only

### Competitor Landscape

- **NerdWallet** dominates 6 of our top 10 target keywords with comprehensive comparison guides
- **Policygenius** has gained 4 positions on average across health insurance terms
- **Investopedia** maintains strong positions on educational insurance content

---

## Technical SEO Findings

### Page Speed
- Insurance Premium Calculator: LCP 2.8s (needs improvement)
- Term Life Estimator: LCP 1.9s (good)
- Health Comparison Tool: LCP 3.4s (poor — heavy JavaScript rendering)

### Schema Markup
- Missing `FAQPage` schema on all insurance calculator pages
- `SoftwareApplication` schema implemented but missing `offers` property
- No `BreadcrumbList` schema on comparison tool pages

### Mobile Usability
- 3 insurance calculator pages flagged for tap target spacing issues
- Health comparison tool table not responsive on screens < 375px

---

## Content Recommendations

1. **Create a comprehensive "Insurance Premium Calculator Guide"** targeting informational queries around how insurance premiums are calculated. Target word count: 2,500+.

2. **Develop comparison content** for "term life vs whole life insurance" with embedded calculator widgets. This addresses the 1,100 monthly search gap identified above.

3. **Add FAQ sections** to all insurance calculator pages to capture People Also Ask features. Priority questions:
   - "How is my insurance premium calculated?"
   - "What factors affect term life insurance rates?"
   - "How to compare health insurance plans effectively?"

4. **Update health insurance comparison tool** content for 2024 open enrollment period. Current content references 2023 plan data.

5. **Build topical authority** with supporting articles:
   - "Understanding Insurance Premium Factors: A Complete Guide"
   - "Term Life Insurance Rates by Age: What to Expect in 2024"
   - "Health Insurance Deductibles Explained for Beginners"
