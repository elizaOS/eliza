# Privacy Policy

## 1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how Eliza Labs, Inc., a Delaware corporation ("Company", "we", "us", "our") collects, uses, and protects your personal information when you use the ElizaOS Platform ("Service").

## 2. Information We Collect

We are committed to being transparent about the data we collect and how we use it to provide our AI agent platform services.

### 2.1 Information You Provide

- Account information (name, email, password)
- Billing and payment information
- Profile information and preferences
- AI agent configurations and prompts
- Training data uploaded for AI agents
- Content you create or upload through the platform
- Communications with our support team
- API usage and integration details

### 2.2 Information Automatically Collected

Through our analytics platform and service monitoring, we collect:

- Usage data and interactions with our Service and AI agents
- Device information (browser type, operating system)
- IP address and general location data
- Session duration and features used
- AI agent performance metrics and usage patterns
- API call logs and response times
- Error logs and debugging information
- System performance and uptime data

## 3. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to:

- Provide and maintain our AI agent platform Service
- Process your transactions and billing
- Send administrative and service-related communications
- Improve our Service and AI agent capabilities
- Analyze usage patterns, performance metrics, and user behavior
- Provide customer support and troubleshooting
- Detect and prevent fraud, abuse, and security incidents
- Train and improve our AI models (with aggregated, anonymized data only)
- Comply with legal obligations and regulatory requirements
- Ensure service security and system integrity

## 4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

### 4.1 Third-Party Service Providers

We may share data with carefully selected third-party providers who assist us in operating our Service:

- Analytics platforms (PostHog, Google Analytics)
- Payment processors (Stripe, PayPal)
- Cloud hosting and infrastructure providers (AWS, Google Cloud)
- Customer support and communication tools
- AI model providers and API services
- Security and monitoring services
- Email and notification services

All third-party providers are bound by confidentiality agreements and data protection requirements.

### 4.2 Legal Requirements

We may disclose information if required by law or in good faith belief that such action is necessary to:

- Comply with legal process or court orders
- Protect and defend our rights and property
- Respond to emergency situations involving personal safety
- Investigate potential violations of our Terms of Service
- Cooperate with law enforcement investigations

## 5. Data Security

We implement comprehensive security measures to protect your data, including:

- End-to-end encryption in transit and at rest
- Multi-factor authentication and access controls
- Regular security audits and penetration testing
- Employee security training and background checks
- Secure API endpoints with rate limiting
- Real-time monitoring and incident response
- Data backup and disaster recovery procedures
- Compliance with industry security standards (SOC 2, ISO 27001)

## 6. Your Rights

Subject to applicable law, you have the right to:

- Access your personal information and AI agent data
- Correct inaccurate or incomplete data
- Request deletion of your personal information
- Object to certain types of processing
- Export your data in a machine-readable format
- Withdraw consent where processing is based on consent
- Restrict processing in certain circumstances
- File complaints with data protection authorities

To exercise these rights, please contact us using the information provided below.

## 7. Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as:

- Your account is active and you continue using our Service
- Needed to provide ongoing AI agent services
- Required by applicable law or regulation
- Necessary for legitimate business purposes (security, legal claims)
- Required for AI model training and improvement (in anonymized form)

Upon account deletion, we will permanently delete your personal information within 30 days, except where retention is required by law.

## 8. International Data Transfers

Your data may be transferred to and processed in countries outside your country of residence, including the United States. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place for such transfers through:

- Standard Contractual Clauses approved by relevant authorities
- Adequacy decisions by competent data protection authorities
- Other legally recognized transfer mechanisms

## 9. AI-Specific Privacy Considerations

When using our AI agent services:

- AI training data may be processed by third-party AI providers
- Agent interactions may be logged for performance monitoring
- We implement privacy-preserving techniques where possible
- You can configure privacy settings for your AI agents
- Sensitive data should be handled according to your own privacy requirements

## 10. Children's Privacy

Our Service is not intended for individuals under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from minors without parental consent.

## 11. Changes to Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically to reflect changes in our practices or applicable law. We will notify you of material changes via email or prominent notice on our Service at least 30 days before they take effect.
